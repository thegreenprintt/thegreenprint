01
Register Your GenesisFX Account
▶

Create your broker account in under 2 minutes.

  • Tap the button above to open the GenesisFX registration page
  • Fill in your full name, email address, and create a password
  • Verify your email when prompted
  • Complete any identity verification steps required
  • Once verified, you’ll land on your GenesisFX Dashboard
Register on GenesisFX →
02
Navigate to TradeLocker Inside GenesisFX
▶

Once logged into your GenesisFX dashboard, here’s how to get to TradeLocker.

  • You’ll be on the main Dashboard screen
  • On mobile, tap the three lines (☰) at the top left to open the menu
  • Find and tap TradeLocker
  • This opens TradeLocker directly connected to your GenesisFX account
On desktop, TradeLocker will appear in the left sidebar or as a direct button on your dashboard.
03
Create a New Demo Account
▶

Inside TradeLocker, create your demo with these exact settings.

  • Tap New Account
  • Change account type from Live Account to Demo Account
  • Name your account: DEMO
  • Keep account at GenFX (default — do not change)
  • Account Type: Standard
  • Leverage: 1:500 (required)
  • Demo Balance: $1,000 or your preferred amount
  • Tap Create Account
Important: Leverage must be set to 1:500. Do not leave it at the default.
04
Check Your Email for TradeLocker Credentials
▶

GenesisFX will automatically email your TradeLocker login details. Save all of these:

  • Login Email
  • Account Number
  • Server Name
  • TradeLocker Password
Can’t find it? Check your spam folder. If it’s still not there after 5 minutes, log back into your GenesisFX dashboard and look for your account credentials there.

Step 06

Log Into
TradeLocker.

Your demo account is created. Now log into TradeLocker using the credentials GenesisFX emailed you.

📧

Have the email from GenesisFX ready before starting this step. It was sent automatically after creating your demo account in Step 5 and contains your account number, server, and password.

01
Open the TradeLocker App
▶
  • Open the TradeLocker app on your phone
  • On the login screen, tap Log In with Broker or Connect Broker
  • Search for or select GenesisFX as your broker
02
Enter Your Credentials from the GenesisFX Email
▶

Use the credentials GenesisFX emailed you after creating your demo account.

  • Enter your Login Email (from the GenesisFX email)
  • Enter the password from the email — this is a TradeLocker password, not your GenesisFX login password
  • For Server, enter: GENFX
  • Tap Log In
Use the password from the email, not your GenesisFX account password. They are two different passwords. Server is GENFX.
03
You’re In — Confirm Your Demo Account
▶
  • You should see your DEMO account with your virtual balance
  • Confirm leverage shows 1:500
  • Tap a chart to explore the platform
  • You’re set up — complete the videos before placing any trades
If you see your demo balance and charts are loading, you’re fully set up. Move on to the next step.
